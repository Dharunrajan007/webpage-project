About Us

Mahendra Engineering College (MEC) is a prominent autonomous engineering institution located in Mallasamudram, Namakkal District, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1995 by Shri M.G. Bharath Kumar, a renowned educationist and philanthropist, MEC aims to provide quality technical education to students from various backgrounds .
